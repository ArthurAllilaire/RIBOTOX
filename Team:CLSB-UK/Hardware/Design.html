Design

The extracted miRNA, in solution, is then amplified isothermally using RPA, to amplify the final result in order to increase precision and make differentiating between positive and negative results easier by amplifying the difference in concentration that needs to be detected.

The amplified miRNA is then added to the second compartment which contains our toehold switches, cell free translation mix and luciferin. The miRNAs bind to the toehold switches, activating them and, hence, triggering the translation of luciferase, which is coded for by the CDS of our toehold switches. The produced luciferase acts on luciferin producing light, which we detect using our self-designed luminometer, which is built into this compartment.
